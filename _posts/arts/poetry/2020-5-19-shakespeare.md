Shall I compare thee to a summer's day? 

Thou art more lovely and more temperate. 



## A Silly Poem 


Said Hamlet to Ophelia, 


I'll draw a sketch of thee, 


What kind of pencil shall I use? 


2B or not 2B? 



By Spike Milligan
